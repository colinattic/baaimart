White Paper

Baai Mart (BAAI) is a decentralized token initially deployed as a BEP-20 asset on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). It is designed as a lightweight, community-driven digital currency focused on fast, secure, and low-cost payments for everyday use.

With a limited total supply of 1.2 million tokens, Baai Mart prioritizes simplicity, security, and accessibility to drive adoption in real-world and online ecosystems.

In the future, Baai Mart plans to expand to own & multiple blockchain networks to increase accessibility and interoperability.
